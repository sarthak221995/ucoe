VISION

Our vision is to be excellent, engaged and accessible. We aim to be recognised for our leadership in:

• Empowering our students to grow their capabilities and transform their lives and of their society.
• Building of technical environment in our university.
• Encouraging support in a wide range of disciplines in various fields of Computer Science.
• Engaging with industry and community to make the world a better place, through the creation, sharing and use of new knowledge.

We will achieve our goals through our distinctive approach to curriculum, the student experience, research and knowledge exchange, and engagement with industry and the community.
