FROM THE DESK


It is a pleasure to lead the department of Computer Engineering. The aim of the department is to pursue excellence in Computer Engineering through teaching and research and we are achieving our objectives with the help of our highly qualified and distinguished faculty. We target to enhance not only their academic knowledge but to nurture their practical skills and provide professional grooming so that our students are ready to serve as they join industry and provide valuable contribution. For this purpose, the department arranges various guest lectures, workshops and ethnical training by industrial professionals so that they come-up as thorough professionals and inspiring individuals.

- DR LAKHWINDER KAUR



VISION

Our vision is to be excellent, engaged and accessible. We aim to be recognised for our leadership in:

• Empowering our students to grow their capabilities and transform their lives and of their society.
• Building of technical environment in our university.
• Encouraging support in a wide range of disciplines in various fields of Computer Science.
• Engaging with industry and community to make the world a better place, through the creation, sharing and use of new knowledge.

We will achieve our goals through our distinctive approach to curriculum, the student experience, research and knowledge exchange, and engagement with industry and the community.
