ICRTC-2016

With great pleasure and delight we would like to inform you that the Department of Computer Engineering, Punjabi University, Patiala (Punjab) is organizing first International Conference titled “INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON RECENT TRENDS IN COMPUTING (ICRTC- 2016)”.

The objective is to bring together the researchers, scientists, engineers and scholar students from various prominent areas of Computing and providing a common platform for sharing the latest research related to the theme of the conference.

Prospective authors are invited to submit full (original) research papers via e-mail to: icrtc16@gmail.com, which are not submitted or published anywhere in other conferences or journals.

All accepted and presented papers will be published online in a special issue of“An International Journal of Engineering Sciences”a refereed online conference proceedings (with ISSN number).
