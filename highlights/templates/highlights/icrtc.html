ICRTC-2016

With great pleasure and delight we would like to inform you that the Department of Computer Engineering, Punjabi University, Patiala (Punjab) is organizing first International Conference titled “INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON RECENT TRENDS IN COMPUTING (ICRTC- 2016)”.

The objective is to bring together the researchers, scientists, engineers and scholar students in all areas of Computing and common platform for sharing the latest research related to the theme of the conference.

Prospective authors are invited to submit full (original) research papers which are not submitted or published or under consideration anywhere in other conferences of journals via e-mail to: icrtc16@gmail.com

Call for Papers

GUIDELINES TO AUTHORS:

Prospective authors are invited to submit original technical papers by the deadline August 2, 2016 for publication in the ICRTC-2016 Conference Proceedings.

It is required that the manuscript follows the standard IEEE camera-ready format (IEEE standard format, double column, 10-point font). Submissions must include title, author and affiliation with email address, abstract and keywords. The IEEE templates in Microsoft Word and LaTeX format can be found at: http://www.ieee.org/conferences_events/conferences/publishing/templates.html. All submissions should be written in English with a maximum paper length of Eight (8) printed pages including figures. Only PDF and DOC files will be accepted for the review process.

All papers will be subjected to peer review by the Technical Program Committee. The Technical Program Committee reserves the right to determine whether a submitted paper is accepted for publication or not. All accepted papers and posters will be published in the proceedings.

All submitted papers will be subjected to a "Similarity (plagiarism) Test" by plagiarism checker. Papers achieving a high similarity score will be examined and those that are deemed unacceptable will be rejected without a formal review.

Paper clearing the similarity test will undergo a full paper review.

Acceptance of papers will be communicated to authors via email.

Accepted papers will be published in ICRTC-2016 proceeding provided that:
• The final version of the accepted paper is prepared strictly as per IEEE format Copyright form is signed;
• At least one author of the accepted paper must register at the conference by paying the appropriate registration fees;
