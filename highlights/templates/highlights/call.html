Call for Papers

The International Conference on Recent Trends in computing (ICRTC-2016) aims to provide a prestigious forum for researchers, scholars, students, academicians and professionals of international repute to have insight in recent advances, new techniques and applications in the field of Computing.

International Conference on Recent Trends in computing will be held in Department of Computer Engineering, Punjabi University, Patiala, and Punjab India during August 30-31, 2016.

Full papers should be sent via e-mail to: icrtc16@gmail.com . All accepted and presented papers will be published online in a special issue of “An International Journal of Engineering Sciences” a refereed online conference proceedings (with ISSN number). Papers submitted in the conference will be peer reviewed. Authors from worldwide are welcome to join us for this conference.
