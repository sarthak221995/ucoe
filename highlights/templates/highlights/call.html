Call for Papers

The International Conference on Recent Trends in computing (ICRTC-2016) aims to provide a high-level international forum for researchers, scholars, students, academicians and professionals to have insight in recent advances, new techniques and applications in the field of Computing.
International Conference on Recent Trends in computing will be held in Department of Computer Engineering, Punjabi University, Patiala, and Punjab India during August 30-31, 2016.

Submitted conference papers will be reviewed by technical committees of the Conference. We sincerely welcome authors from worldwide to join us for this conference ICRTC-2016 invites original contributions on all topics related to Computational techniques, including but not limited to:
