About HACKODERS

Hackoders is a group of passionate students of Department of Computer Engineering, Punjabi university. The aim of the group is to contribute in the technical development and spread awareness about the latest trends in computer technology among the students.

Along with organizing various events from time to time, it plans to impart coaching to the interested students in various technical languages. The group will not only help in updating knowledge but also in improving the technical skills of the students so that they may be better equipped in the time to come

Let us see if you are smart enough to identify the hacker. The event has two rounds:

ROUND 1

• 30 multiple-choice questions based latest tech trends, aptitude and programming.

• Separate question sets for students of different years.

• Top 5 teams from each year will move to next round



ROUND 2

Programming based questions whose answers act as an input to an android application. After submitting your answers, name of the hacker will appear on the screen. The one with the most accurate answer in least time will be declared as the winner.

• For First Year – C based questions

• For Second / Third Year – C++ / JAVA
