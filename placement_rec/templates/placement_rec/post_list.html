MESSAGE FROM PLACEMENT CELL

We at placement cell always put all our efforts to satisfy the students’ expectations for their future career goals. We organizes various guest lectures, seminars and workshops where students and faculty members learn from the practical experiences of personalities from various fields of industry. The college encourages the students to start their own enterprises after completion of their course. In this context the college is having an Entrepreneurship and Skill Development Cell.We organize pre-placement activities from day one and students are trained on aspects like:
• Facing Interviews
• Facing Group Discussions
• Professional Resume Writing
• Cracking Aptitude Test
• Internship Projects
• Skill Development Sessions and Programs
• Recruitment and Placement Sessions
• Programmes on Soft Skills

 In a nutshell, the idea of placement cell is to prepare graduates while at college, who are not only well equipped in engineering subjects and software engineering fundamentals, but also have the ability to integrate the learning across the foundation areas, to apply them in practical situations.


-GAURAVDEEP SHARMA
Placement Nominee
