MESSAGE FROM PLACEMENT CELL

Training is the process of acquiring the essential skills required for a certain job. The students get a chance to learn new technologies practically rather than rely on theory. Department helps the students to arrange their six weeks and six months training in reputed companies like Infosys, HCL, HP etc. Department also offer House Training Program for B.Tech and M.Tech students where students will be taught different programming languages and will be able to work on short term live projects. It is very important for students to get trained properly for their better career.

We at placement cell always put all our efforts to satisfy the students’ expectations for their future career goals. We organizes various guest lectures, seminars and workshops where students and faculty members learn from the practical experiences of personalities from various fields of industry. The department of computer engineering encourages the students to start their own enterprises after completion of their course. In this context the engineering wing is having an Entrepreneurship and Skill Development Cell.

We organize pre-placement activities from day one and students are trained on aspects like:
• Facing Interviews
• Facing Group Discussions
• Professional Resume Writing
• Cracking Aptitude Test
• Internship Projects
• Skill Development Sessions and Programs
• Recruitment and Placement Sessions
• Programmes on Soft Skills

 In a nutshell, the idea of placement cell is to prepare graduates who are not only well equipped in engineering subjects but also have the ability to integrate the learning across the foundation areas, to apply them in practical situations.
