MISSION

The Department of Computer Engineering is committed to build Punjabi University an internationally significant research university, with undergraduate, graduate and professional programs of excellent quality. The department also has a mission to create an exceptional learning environment that advances a civil and sustainable society, and supports outstanding research to serve the people.

Punjabi University

It was established on the 30 April 1962, and is only the second university in the world to be named after a language,

Punjabi University teaches and researches in science, engineering and technology, humanities, social sciences, performing arts and sports.

The university campus is spread over an area of 316 acres.The institution has 65 teaching and research departments, covering disciplines in Humanities, Science, Fine Arts, Computer Science and Business Management.
